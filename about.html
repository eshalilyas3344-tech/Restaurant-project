Our Story

Authentic Italian cuisine with a family tradition since 1995

A Family Legacy

Founded in 1995 by Giovanni and Maria Rossi, Bella Vista began as a small family trattoria in the heart of Florence. What started as a humble restaurant serving traditional Tuscan recipes has grown into an award-winning dining destination, while still maintaining its family-owned charm.

For over 25 years, we've been committed to bringing authentic Italian flavors to our community. Our recipes have been passed down through three generations of the Rossi family, each adding their own touch while respecting tradition.

In 2010, Giovanni's son, Chef Antonio Rossi, took over the kitchen after training in some of Italy's most prestigious restaurants. He combines traditional techniques with modern innovation, creating dishes that honor our heritage while exciting the modern palate.

Meet Our Chef

Antonio Rossi

Executive Chef & Owner

Born into the restaurant business, Chef Antonio Rossi began learning family recipes at his grandmother's knee. After formal culinary training at the prestigious ALMA Cooking School in Parma, Italy, he worked in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe before returning home to lead Bella Vista's kitchen.

Chef Antonio's philosophy is simple: "Respect the ingredients, honor tradition, but don't be afraid to innovate." His signature dishes blend classic Italian flavors with contemporary techniques, creating memorable dining experiences.
